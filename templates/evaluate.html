Methodology
  • Train/Test split: 80/20 stratified by user ID. Seed = 42.
  • Relevance threshold: Rating ≥ 7 is considered a "liked" book.
  • RMSE: Root Mean Squared Error on rating predictions for the test set.
  • Precision@10: Fraction of top-10 recommendations that are relevant, averaged across test users.
  • NDCG@10: Normalised Discounted Cumulative Gain at rank 10 — rewards correct items ranked higher.
  • Sentiment correction: VADER compound score applied as soft rating adjustment (α=0.5) before training.
